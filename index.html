How to Play

98th Academy Awards · Watch Party Ballot 2026

🎬
Step 1 — Create the Room
Enter your name and leave the Room Code field blank. Hit "Enter the Room ✦" — a unique 6-letter code is generated just for your party.
🔗
Step 2 — Share the Room Code
Copy the link or 6-letter code shown after room creation and send it to your friends via WhatsApp, iMessage, or any chat.
🗳️
Step 3 — Lock In Your Picks
Go through all 24 categories, pick your winner for each, then tap "Lock In My Picks ✦" before the show begins.
📺
Step 4 — Reveal Winners Live
As each award is announced on TV, find that category, select the winner from the gold "✦ HOST" bar, and tap "Reveal". Everyone's ballot updates instantly.
🔄
Step 5 — Mistakes? No Problem
Accidentally revealed the wrong winner? Tap "Clear" next to it and reveal the correct one — scores correct automatically.
💡 Host tip: Keep the app open throughout the show. Reveal each winner as it's announced for maximum drama — the leaderboard updates live for everyone!
📱
Step 1 — Open the Ballot
Open the link shared by your host, or go to the ballot URL directly on your phone or laptop.
🔑
Step 2 — Enter Name & Room Code
Type your name and enter the Room Code shared by the host. Tap "Enter the Room ✦" to join the party.
🗳️
Step 3 — Make Your Picks & Lock In
Pick your winner for all 24 categories, then tap "Lock In My Picks ✦" before the show starts. You can't change predictions once locked.
🏆
Step 4 — Watch, Score & Win
As the host reveals each winner, your ballot shows ✓ Correct or ✗ Wrong instantly. Check the Leaderboard tab anytime to see live standings.
💡 Remote friends welcome! You can be in a different city — as long as you use the same room code you'll see the same live leaderboard as everyone else.
➕
Scoring — +1 Per Correct Pick
Every time you correctly predict a winner, you earn +1 point. There are 24 categories, so the maximum possible score is 24 points.
📊
Live Leaderboard
The Leaderboard tab updates in real time the moment a winner is revealed. Everyone in the room — across the couch or across the country — sees the same standings simultaneously.
🔒
Lock In Before the Show
All players must tap "Lock In My Picks ✦" before the ceremony starts. Picks are fixed once locked — no changing answers after hearing winners on TV!
🌍
Play from Anywhere
Works on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop. Friends in different cities can join the same room using the room code and compete on the same live leaderboard.
Most correct predictions wins · Scores auto-calculated · No manual tracking needed
